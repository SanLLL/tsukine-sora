About Tsukine Sora

Tsukine Sora is a character that has the essence of the peacefulness that outer space contains. She has a deep navy and glowing halo inspired design, and when you examine her, she has a powerful, but calm aura. She has long braided twin-tails, galaxy dark theme outfit, and cyan luminescent accents.
