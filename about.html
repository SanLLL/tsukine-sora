About Tsukine Sora

Tsukine Sora embodies the quiet serenity of outer space. With deep navy tones and a glowing halo motif, she carries a serene yet powerful presence. Her design includes long braided twin-tails, a dark galaxy-themed outfit, and cyan luminescent accents.
